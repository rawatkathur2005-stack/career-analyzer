Interview Tips

  • STAR Method: Structure your answers: Situation, Task, Action, and Result.
  • Quantify Impact: Do not just state tasks. Say: "...resulting in 30% speedups" or "...saving 10 engineering hours".
  • Reference Tools: Explicitly mention tools from your resume stack (e.g. Git, React, SQL) to show command.
